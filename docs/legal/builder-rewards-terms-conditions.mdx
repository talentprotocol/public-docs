## 1. Program Overview

The Program automatically distributes rewards to eligible builders in the Base ecosystem based on their verified onchain and open-source contributions. The program distributes 5 ETH for every 500 builders each month.

## 3. Reward Structure

Weekly rewards are distributed across three tiers:

a) Tier 1: Top 25 builders share 30% of the reward pool, proportionally to their activity and impact.

b) Tier 2: Next 75 builders share 30% of the reward pool, proportionally to their activity and impact.

c) Tier 3: Next 150 builders share 20% of the reward pool, proportionally to their activity and impact.

d) Tier 4: Next 250 builders share 20% of the reward pool, proportionally to their activity and impact.

## 4. Evaluation Criteria

Your ranking and rewards are determined by three separate factors:

a) Verified contributions to public repositories on that month

b) Activity on verified contracts you've deployed on Base on that month

c) Earned Farcaster Mini App rewards on that month

The specific weights and formulas used to calculate rankings are proprietary and subject to change at the sole discretion of Talent Protocol.
