The Program consists of three (3) distinct challenges.

- Distribution Date: Rewards will be distributed on December 16, 2025.

3.2 Second Challenge

## 4. Reward Structure

Rewards for each challenge are distributed across three tiers:

Tier 1: Top 10 builders share 50% of the reward pool, proportionally to their activity and impact.
Tier 2: Next 15 builders share 25% of the reward pool, proportionally to their activity and impact.
Tier 3: Next 25 builders share 25% of the reward pool, proportionally to their activity and impact.
